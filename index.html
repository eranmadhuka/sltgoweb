sltgo

Partnering with FON, the world’s largest community Wi-Fi network, SLT brings you sltgo. With sltgo, now you can carry your SLT home broadband connection wherever you go. You can use your SLT home broadband credentials to access Wi-Fi from any sltgo Wi-Fi hotspot locally or over 23 million FON’s community Wi-Fi hotspot overseas. sltgo mobile app will automatically connect you to the nearest available Wi-Fi hotspot so you are always connected to the island’s fastest broadband network.

sltgo will create two Wi-Fi networks in your home broadband router, the private one to be used by you and another for public sltgo consumers. The number of people who can use your home hotspot at a time is limited and subject to a maximum speed limitation giving priority to your private network, so your broadband experience will remain almost the same as before even after subscribing to this solution.

Is it secure?

Your home private Wi-Fi signal is separate from the sltgo or Fon public signals. This means your data and information will be kept secure from public users.

Like most public Wi-Fi networks, this solution is an unencrypted and open network and security limitations of such a network prevails in this solution as well.

Will my internet speed and data quota reduce?

You will be sharing only up to 2Mbps maximum speed for the Public SSID only if a public user is connected to your home connection. It will have only a minimal impact on your own Internet browsing speed.

The data used by public users are not consumed from your home broadband data allowance, so your monthly data quota will not be affected.
